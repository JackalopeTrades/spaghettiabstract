Heed Human Am I Catalogue of Carnage Building Blocks for a Spaceship We Made Our Own Universe

Heed is made up of five original musical compositions. Each of which are written, produced and mixed by Shane M Ryan. Mastered by Giancarlo Roberti. The instruments involved with the making of this album are piano, violin, synth, coins, cardboard, scissors, various noises and the experimental use of reverbs & delays. The entire mood of the album is based around the genres of Contemporary Classical, Ambient & Minimalism.

Release Date: September 7th 2019
